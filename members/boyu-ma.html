Boyu Ma

Postdoctoral Researcher

Dr. Ma is a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at Nanyang Technological University, working with Prof. Jianfei Yang. He received the China National Scholarship (Top 0.1%) among other honors, and served as a Torchbearer for the 2021 FISU World University Games. His research interests include robotics and embodied AI.
